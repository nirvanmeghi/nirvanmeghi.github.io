Identity, Values and Strengths Exploration

Identity & Values

I didn't explore much with my Identity & Values before this. Or I was just unaware of the fact that how much of me is because of the way I believe in myself and see myself in this world.
I was born in Nepal, a small himalayan country, a landlocked country. So living in New Zealand near beautiful beaches is something that I find very unusual . The hills, mountains & valleys of Nepal have so much ingrained in my memory that every night when I go to sleep I go back to those places in my dreams. It's very rare that I don’t dream about it. It’s like I have a place in my subconscious that I have to go there every night to get recharged.
As I grow older with a lot of life experiences, my identity and values have also been evolving. I aspired to be someone like Niropa or Tilopa at the same time I strive for materialistic things. So it's a bit conflicting. Art is my escape from reality and anything with nature, I fall in love !


Strengths and Limitations

My strength is perseverance. Sometimes even though everything goes the other way. I see the bigger picture and try to find the solution. I tend not to be afraid to do new things. Mainly open to others.
My limitation is listening to my self doubting voice.
