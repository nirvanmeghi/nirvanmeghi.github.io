About Me

Identity, Values and Strengths Exploration

Identity & Values

This is one of the very abstract parts of the personality I have never given time to think about. I don't have any definitive answers to my Identity & Values.

I was born in Nepal, a small himalayan country, a landlocked country. So living in New Zealand is something that I find very fascinating. I have grown in the base of hills, mountains & valleys, so every night when I go to sleep I go back to those places in my dreams. It's very rare that I don’t dream about it. It’s like I have a place in my subconscious that I have to go there every night to get recharge.

I was a very curious kid, very introverted but spiritual. Very dependent on parents. My teenage years were the most challenging part of my life. I think I'm trying to find some healing now. As a teenager I struggled with my identity although when I was alone with nature I used to be one with the river & trees, those experiences were surreal, back then I had definitely more eagerness to live & now I strive to be that again.

So my Identity & Values are ever changing as I grow and evolve.
Strengths and Limitations


My strength is perseverance. Sometimes even though everything goes the other way. I see the bigger picture and try to find the solution. I tend not to be afraid to do new things. Mainly open to others.
My limitation is listening to my self doubting voice.
